Shortcode is not Rendered as a Widget

Sometimes, visual editors in your CMS will modify the shortcode without you noticing. We are always working on making sure all major editors are supported.

Supported editors

Currently, shortcodes are known to work well with the following editor:

  • Plain textarea (no editor enabled)
  • JCE
  • RokPad
  • TinyMCE
  • CodeMirror
  • Ace

Not supported

Not supported at the moment, but coming soon:

  • CK editor
